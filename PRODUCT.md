# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Independent film enthusiasts and early-adopter film creators who care about cinema as craft. They encounter this page via social discovery or word-of-mouth — they aren't searching a catalog, they're being introduced. They're skeptical of streaming monoculture and would find Mubi or A24 more credible than Netflix as a recommendation source. On any given visit, they're deciding: *is this worth my attention?*

## Product Purpose

Sunsetrider Media is building a pay-to-view platform for independent filmmakers to host and distribute their work directly to audiences. The current landing page is a pre-launch awareness surface: a split-screen hero showcasing two films (Killing Lionel I/II, Card Dead II/II), social links, and an email signup. Success means visitors engage with the trailers, follow on Instagram, and leave feeling they've discovered something rare.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic · Intimate · Independent

Voice: Directorial. Speaks with quiet authority — never loud, never corporate. Copy is spare and deliberate, like a film title card. The brand earns attention rather than demanding it. It is confident enough to leave silence in the room.

## Anti-references

- **Netflix / mainstream streaming platforms** — no row-of-thumbnails grid, no bold red, no progress bars, no "content" language. This is not a catalog.
- **Generic tech startups** — no hero metrics, no feature comparison tables, no soft gradients with sans-serif stats.
- **Over-produced film distributor sites** — no bombastic poster treatments, no busy movie-poster typography stacking.

## Design Principles

1. **The film commands the frame.** Every layout decision gives way to the footage. UI exists to frame the work, not compete with it.
2. **Restraint as confidence.** What's left out is as deliberate as what's in. Empty space is not a gap to fill.
3. **A platform with taste.** Neutral is invisible. Sunset Rider has a point of view and the design reflects it — dark, curated, unhurried.
4. **Discovery, not browsing.** Visitors should feel they've found something rare, not scrolled through a shelf.
5. **One room.** The landing page is a single orchestrated experience — not sections stitched together, but one mood sustained from top to bottom.

## Visual Identity

**Brand cream:** `#fcf6ed` → `oklch(0.975 0.0135 78.2)` — warm cream from the logo. Used for the "SUNSETRIDER" wordmark text and email button label.

**Brand accent:** `#cf2803` → `oklch(0.553 0.205 32.4)` — deep red-orange. Used for "MEDIA" wordmark, series indicators, play button hover/focus, email input focus border, CTA button background, and all focus outlines.

**Type:** Barlow Condensed (display/UI labels, 600–800 weight) + Barlow (body, 400–500 weight).

**Color strategy:** Committed dark — near-black canvas (`oklch(0.08 0 0)`) with the cream accent at ≤15% surface coverage. All supplementary neutrals are hue-tinted toward the brand.

**Logo:** `public/newlogo.png` (1254×1254, square). Rendered in the navbar at 40×40px alongside the stacked wordmark.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific WCAG level required. Apply WCAG AA as a sensible baseline: 4.5:1 contrast for body text, keyboard-navigable interactive elements, descriptive alt text. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for any animation.
